Gracious designs is a party Decorator and Events Management Company based in Kathmandu, Nepal.

  • We are best known as Theme party decorator and we prioritised quality and uniqueness on our decorations.
  • We have worked on various themes for occasions like Birthdays , Baby Shower , Pasni , Anniversary , Baby welcoming and Gender Reveal etc .
  • Be it a small , intimate celebration or a big corporate setup, we have always kept tidiness , visual appeals , quality and finishing as our topmost priority .

After being a part of more than 1000+ Events we have curated packages that are most loved and ordered by our clients that makes it a tad bit easier for our clients to know our services and prices better. However , like our utmost priority has been trying out unique ideas we are always up for new themes , trends , ideas and we absolutely love bringing those ideas to life by our decorations.
